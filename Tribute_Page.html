Tribute to Thor - The God of Thunder

Thor, the son of Odin and protector of Asgard, is one of the strongest warriors in the Marvel universe. Known for his bravery, strength, and his legendary hammer Mjolnir, Thor stands as a symbol of honor and justice.

“Whosoever holds this hammer, if he be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor.”

About Thor

Thor first appeared in Marvel Comics in 1962. As the God of Thunder, he defends both Asgard and Earth from powerful enemies. His strength, leadership, and compassion make him one of the most loved heroes. He inspires courage and reminds everyone that true power comes with responsibility.
